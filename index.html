🌿 A Note on Shared Living

As with most multi-unit residential buildings, our shared common areas — hallways, stairwells, and corridors — are used by many residents daily. Occasionally, an insect may find its way in, particularly during warmer months. This is completely normal for buildings of this nature.

Please know that we take cleanliness very seriously. Your unit is thoroughly cleaned and sanitized before every stay, and we continuously do our best to ensure a fresh and comfortable environment for all our guests.

If you ever notice anything that concerns you during your stay, please don't hesitate to reach out to us — we're always here and happy to help. 🙏
